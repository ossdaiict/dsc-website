Welcome to DSC DA-IICT

Developer Student Clubs is a Google Developers program for university students to learn software development. We are a group of people enthusiastic about technology, development and open source. We are a group passionate about sharing the same enthusiasm with people who wish to introduce, teach, develop and associate with the very same technology that none of us can live without.

What We Do?

Info Sessions

Provide a step to step guide sessions on approaching any tech stack or from a range of topics.

Hands-on Workshop

The real fun where the members will show you how to proceed on different app creation.

Semester Long Projects

SLoP is an initiative by us to help students to get into Open Source Software world.

Code Dementia

We maintain a tech blog Code Dementia on Medium, covering beginner to advanced level topics!

How to participate and grow with us?

  1. Follow our social media to stay updated! (links given in the footer :P)
  2. Join our WhatsApp community group and participate in the discussions. Ask your doubts or share cool stuff!
  3. Attend our sessions, workshops and programs like SLoP. We provide resources with emails.
  4. Share your articles with us to publish on Code Dementia, and contribute to our GitHub organization OSS DA-IICT.
